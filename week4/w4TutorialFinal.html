Heart Lake

The Lake that will steal your heart!

Heart Lake Conservation Area is located in Brampton, Ontario, Canada. The lake is famously known for being a heart shape. The 43 acre lake boasts wildlife, great views and serenity. There are many fun activities here, wether you are looking for an adventurous date with some fishing and treetop trekking, or if you want something more chilled, you can take a picnic lake side and soak up a beautiful sunset.
